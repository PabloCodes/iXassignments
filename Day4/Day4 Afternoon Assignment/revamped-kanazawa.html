Kanazawa

I was in Kanazawa for 2 days and saw a lot of really cool things. Kanazawa is the capital of the Ishikawa Prefecture which is more or less in the middle of Japan's main island. Kanazawa is well known for its arts because prior to the Meiji Restoration in 1868 the Samurai in the area were relatively wealthy and could afford to be patrons of arts. Kanazawa is specifically known for it's ceramic pottery style, lacquerware and silk dyeing.
